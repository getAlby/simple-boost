Example: Fiat Amoutns

You can define the amounts in fiat currency (e.g. usd, eur, gbp, etc.) The amount will be converted on the fly to satoshis to generate the lightning invoice.

HTML

<simple-boost
  currency="usd"
  amount="5"
  theme="gumroad"
  address="hello@getalby.com"
></simple-boost>
